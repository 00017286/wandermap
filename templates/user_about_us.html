About Us

About Me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Polina, and I'm passionate about travelling. With a deep interest in documenting and sharing my journeys, I've dedicated myself to develop a platform where travellers can become a part of world community by creating their travel maps. I believe in the power of technology and creativity to make a real difference, and I'm constantly learning to enhance my expertise.

Mission and Values

My mission is to help people shift from old-fashioned paper to convenient and modern digital diaries when it comes to their trips. I am deeply committed to integrity and innovation and believe that true success comes from making a meaningful impact. Whether it's through coding or travelling the world, I am always focused on delivering results that matter to both individuals and communities.

Personal Philosophy

I believe that the greatest character development is gained through new experiences, which drives me to approach every challenge with curiosity. Outside of work, I enjoy searching the net to add new places to my bucket list, and I find that this activity helps me stay inspired and open to the bigger picture. My journey is ongoing, and I look forward to sharing more of my story with you as it evolves.

Contact Me

I'm always open to new ideas, collaborations, and opportunities to connect with like-minded individuals. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or just want to say hello. You can contact me at polinadolgopolova@gmail.com or follow my work on instagram.
